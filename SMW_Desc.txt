[h1]Show Me Whales[/h1]
Super simple tiny mod. Stackable. 
Makes Space Whales visible on any map. 
That's it. That's what it does.

Oh.. I did put an INI in case anyone has some other whales they want to make visible too. 

[code]
[ShowMeWhales]
OtherWhales=/Game/Mods/SomeOtherMod1/Some/Path/OtherWhale1.OtherWhale1,/Game/Mods/SomeOtherMod2/Some/Path/OtherWhale2.OtherWhale2
[/code]
